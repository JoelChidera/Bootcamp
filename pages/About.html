The Bootcamp

This progarm is designed to equip young minds with essiential tech skills, preparing them for our faced-paced, technology-driven future.
With extensive experience in tech-nology, we understand that young people often face challenges in choosing a career path.
Therefore, we aim to address the gap in career guidance and skill acquisition through our program.
The progam is divid-ed into two categories:"The Genius Track,"running throughout July, and"The Marvel Track,"
starting in August. These tracks are available across all our branches and also offered online for remote participants.
